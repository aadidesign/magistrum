## Bookable slot windows

- Monday to Saturday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM Gulf Standard Time (GST, UTC+04:00) — for discovery calls
- Sunday closed
- Avoid suggesting slots later than 6 PM unless the client specifically asks

## Duration per call type

- Discovery call: 45 minutes (free, no obligation)
- Scoping call: 60 minutes (after discovery, before written scope)
- Training session: 60-180 minutes depending on programme

## Information to collect per booking

For any discovery call, capture:

1. Name (first + last is fine)
2. UAE phone number (WhatsApp preferred)
3. Service of interest (Books, CRM, Workplace, Finance Suite, Zoho One, or Training)
4. Preferred time window (e.g. "Tuesday or Wednesday afternoon next week")
5. Optional: current accounting/CRM system, team size, UAE entity type (mainland vs. free zone)

## Confirmation

After capturing the info, the chatbot should:
- Confirm you've got the details.
- Set expectation: a Magistrum consultant will call back within 1 working day during business hours.
- Offer WhatsApp as an alternative for faster contact.

## No-show policy

If a confirmed call is missed without notice, we'll attempt to reschedule once. After two missed slots we close the enquiry — the form is always open if they come back.

## What NOT to promise in chat

- Specific times "we're free at 2pm on Thursday" — we don't have live calendar access in the bot. Always say "we'll confirm a time when we call back".
- Discount codes or promotional pricing — we don't run promotions in this niche.
- Filing services for VAT or Corporate Tax submission — we configure the system, the client (or their auditor) submits. Be clear about this distinction.
