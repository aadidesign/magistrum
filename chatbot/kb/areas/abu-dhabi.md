## Coverage

Zoho implementation and training for Abu Dhabi mainland and Free Zone businesses.

## Neighbourhoods we work in

Khalifa City, Mussafah, Yas Island, Al Reem Island, Masdar City, Abu Dhabi Global Market (ADGM), KIZAD (Khalifa Industrial Zone).

## Notes

- ADGM clients face additional data protection considerations (ADGM DP Regulations 2021). We configure data residency and access controls accordingly.
- Onsite visits scheduled as needed; remote delivery for the majority of phases.
