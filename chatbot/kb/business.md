## What Magistrum actually does

The practice is intentionally broad. The five service lines that show on magistrum.net are:

1. **Digital Marketing**, including custom website design and development, optimised landing pages, search engine optimisation, Google Ads, Facebook Ads, funnel design, and online course launch support for coaches and consultants.
2. **IT Consultancy**, advising businesses on how to digitise their operations using the right technology.
3. **Software Development**, building scalable custom solutions with minimal infrastructure complexity and operational cost.
4. **Online Accounting Classes**, delivered through the India entity, covering Zoho Books, Tally Prime and broader accounting fundamentals.
5. **Zoho Books and the wider Zoho ecosystem**, where Magistrum is an Authorised Partner. Implementation, training and ongoing support across Zoho Books, Zoho CRM, Zoho Workplace, Zoho Finance Suite and Zoho One.

The India entity additionally operates Bilzen Chocolates and Confectioneries as a separate non-IT business. Cross-sector depth, single ownership.

## Positioning for this pitch site

The pitch site emphasises the Zoho Authorised Partner positioning because that is the angle Magistrum's Google profile leads with and where buyer demand is currently highest in the Dubai market. The other service lines are visible on the site under "the wider practice".

A useful one-liner: live on Zoho in days, not months, with a Dubai head office plus a Mumbai practice backing you afterwards.

## What makes Magistrum different

- A Zoho Authorised Partner that also runs Authorised Tally Prime training, useful for clients moving off Tally.
- Cross-border presence in Dubai plus Mumbai. One team, one accountability, useful for UAE businesses with back-office or supplier operations in India.
- A dual model: implementation engagements for businesses, certification training for individuals and finance teams. The two halves inform each other.
- A finance-led co-founder pairing (Haridas on the finance side, Seema on the IT side) which keeps the practice grounded in real accounting and IT discipline rather than purely sales.

## What Magistrum is not

- Not a generalist body shop. Implementation work is focused on the Zoho ecosystem and adjacent migrations from Tally, QuickBooks, Sage or Excel.
- Not the cheapest partner in the market. The practice competes on methodology, training quality and post-go-live support.

## Engagement model

Every engagement begins with a free 45-minute discovery call. The output is a written summary of recommended scope and timeline. Engagements only start after a written, signed scope and pricing document. Fixed scope, fixed timeline, fixed price wherever possible.

## Payment posture

Specific pricing is confirmed in writing after discovery. Indicative engagement ranges live in the FAQ entries. The Magistrum group accepts bank transfer (most common B2B in the UAE), cheque, and card for smaller training engagements.
