## Pricing and quotes

### How much does Zoho Books implementation cost?

Most UAE SME implementations run AED 5,500 to AED 18,000 depending on scope (single module vs. Finance Suite, simple migration vs. complex multi-branch). The Zoho Books licence itself is paid directly to Zoho, from around AED 60 per user per month. We confirm a fixed-scope quote in writing after the free 45-minute discovery call — no scope creep.

### How much does Zoho CRM implementation cost?

Typically AED 7,500 to AED 22,000 depending on complexity of the sales process and number of users.

### How much does a Zoho One rollout cost?

Implementation typically AED 35,000 to AED 95,000 across a 6-12 week phased rollout. Zoho One licence from around AED 165 per user per month from Zoho.

### Do you have transparent pricing or a price list?

We share fixed-scope quotes in writing after discovery. The reason we don't publish a price list is that engagements vary widely by scope and we'd rather give you an honest number than a misleading menu.

### Do you offer a free consultation?

Yes. The first 45 minutes is always free and includes a written summary of what we'd do and what we wouldn't.

## Timeframes

### How long does Zoho Books implementation take?

7 to 14 working days for most UAE SMEs. We've done 5-day go-lives for simple setups and 4-week migrations for complex multi-branch operations.

### How long does Zoho CRM implementation take?

10 to 21 working days. The longer end is when the sales process needs substantial mapping or there's data to clean from a previous CRM.

### How quickly can we get a discovery call?

Usually within 1-2 working days. WhatsApp tends to be the fastest route.

## Guarantees and warranties

### Do you guarantee go-live by a specific date?

We commit to a date in writing before we start. If we miss it because of something on our side, we make it right — usually free extra training or extended support.

### What if we're not happy?

You always have a written scope. If a deliverable in that scope isn't met, we redo it. We're a small enough practice that our reputation matters more than any one engagement.

## Emergency and out-of-hours

### Do you offer 24/7 support?

No. We're available Mon-Sat 9-9 GST. For genuine emergencies (e.g. unable to invoice, blocked VAT return on a deadline), retainer clients have escalation paths.

### What if our VAT return is due tomorrow?

WhatsApp us as soon as you realise. Even if you're not yet a client, we'll point you at the right next step.

## Insurance and payment

### Do you accept card / bank transfer / cheque?

Bank transfer is most common. We accept cheque (still standard B2B in UAE) and card for smaller training engagements. Full payment terms appear on the signed scope.

### When do we pay?

Typically 50% on signing, 50% on go-live. Adjusted for longer Zoho One rollouts.

### Do you have a VAT TRN?

Yes — it appears on every invoice we issue and in the website footer.

## How we work

### Are you really a Zoho Authorised Partner?

Yes. We're a Zoho Authorised Training and Implementation Partner. Verifiable on the Zoho Partner Directory.

### Do you work onsite or remote?

Onsite is standard for Dubai engagements. For other emirates, we work remote-first with periodic site visits. Either way, training is always live (online or in-person).

### Who do we deal with day-to-day?

Each engagement has one named lead consultant who's your single point of contact. You won't be passed between account managers.

### Do you do work outside the Zoho stack?

Our parent practice in India also covers Tally Prime, SAP FICO, and general accounting training. For the UAE entity, we focus on Zoho — that focus is part of why we're good at it.
