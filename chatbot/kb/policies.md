## Engagement

Every engagement begins with a free 45-minute discovery call. The output is a written summary of what we'd do and what we wouldn't. No engagement begins until a written scope and price is signed by both sides.

## Pricing

Pricing is confirmed in writing after discovery. We don't publish public price lists because engagements vary too widely; we'd rather give you an honest range and then a fixed number than a misleading menu.

## Payment

Typically 50% on signing, 50% on go-live for implementation engagements. Training is paid in advance for individuals, on standard 30-day terms for corporate cohorts. Methods accepted: bank transfer (most common), cheque, card (for smaller engagements).

## Scope changes

If you want to add to scope mid-engagement, we tell you the impact on price and timeline in writing before any new work begins. We don't surprise-invoice you for "scope creep".

## Cancellation

Within 7 days of signing: full refund minus any time we've already spent. After that: pro-rated to work delivered to date.

## Confidentiality

Standard mutual NDA is part of every engagement, signed at scoping. We never share client data, lists, or work product with third parties.

## Data residency

Where you need data to remain in the UAE region (e.g. DIFC, ADGM, or general PDPL preference), we configure Zoho's regional data residency from setup. Confirm during discovery if this applies to you.

## Complaints

If anything isn't right, the fastest route is the named lead consultant on your engagement. If that doesn't resolve it, info@magistrum.net escalates directly to the leadership team — we read it.
