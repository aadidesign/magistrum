## Overview

Online Accounting Classes are delivered through Magistrum's India entity, Magistrum Corpserve Private Limited. The programme is built for individuals and professionals who want practical accounting skills, including the systems most-used in the UAE and India: Zoho Books and Tally Prime.

## What's included

- **Practical Zoho Books training**, delivered by an Authorised Zoho training centre.
- **Tally Prime training and certification**, delivered by an Authorised Tally training centre.
- Hands-on practice with case studies and example datasets.
- Online and offline delivery, depending on geography and preference.
- Optional add-ons covering office automation and tax simulation.
- Certificate on completion.

## How we work

The programme is designed to be accessible to recent graduates and working professionals alike. It is engaging, practical and outcome-led: the aim is real, employable skill, not theoretical coverage.

## Common questions

### Is this online, offline, or both?

Both options are available. Many learners take it online; in-person sessions run from the India practice.

### Is this run by the Dubai team or the India team?

The accounting training programme is run by the India entity, Magistrum Corpserve Private Limited. The UAE entity surfaces it as part of the wider Magistrum offering.

### Is the certificate recognised?

Yes. Magistrum is an Authorised Zoho training centre (for Zoho Books) and an Authorised Tally Prime training centre, and certificates are issued under those authorisations.

### Do you guarantee jobs?

The India practice publicly mentions a Lifetime Job Assurance approach on its accounting programmes. Specifics are confirmed during enrolment. Treat job assurance as a programme feature on the India side; the UAE entity refers learners to the India programme for these courses.
