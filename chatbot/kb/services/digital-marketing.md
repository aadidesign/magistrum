## Overview

Digital Marketing is one of Magistrum's five active service lines, listed publicly on magistrum.net. The aim is to help businesses generate visibility, traffic and qualified leads, then convert that traffic into customers through a measured, iterative approach.

## What's included

- **Custom website design and development**: bespoke, brand-led websites with responsive development, mobile-first delivery and SEO foundations baked in.
- **Optimised landing pages**: campaign-specific pages engineered for conversion, with thoughtful design, focused copywriting, A/B testing and mobile-first delivery.
- **Search Engine Optimisation (SEO)**: a full programme covering keyword research, on-page optimisation, technical fixes, ethical link-building and local SEO where relevant. Regular reports keep the work transparent.
- **Google Ads**: keyword research, ad creative, landing page alignment, retargeting and ongoing campaign management with transparent performance reporting.
- **Facebook Ads**: campaign strategy, creative production, audience segmentation, A/B testing, retargeting and clear reporting.
- **Funnel design**: mapping the customer journey end-to-end, designing landing pages and follow-up sequences that match each stage, plus lead-magnet design and post-conversion nurture.
- **Online course launches for coaches and consultants**: from course outline and platform selection through launch-day support and post-launch optimisation.

## How we work

We start with a real conversation about the business, goals and audience. The strategy is built around the actual customer journey, not a templated funnel. We test, measure and iterate, and we report transparently so the client sees what is and is not working.

## Common questions

### Do you do websites, or just marketing?

Both, in fact websites are a core part of the digital marketing offer. Many engagements begin with a website rebuild before paid traffic starts.

### Do you offer SEO and Google Ads together?

Yes. SEO and paid media are usually more effective together than separately, especially for UAE SMEs in competitive niches.

### Can you help us launch an online course?

Yes. Magistrum has a dedicated online course launch offer for coaches and consultants, covering structure, platform, marketing and launch-day support.
