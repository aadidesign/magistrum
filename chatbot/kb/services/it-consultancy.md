## Overview

IT Consultancy is one of Magistrum's five service lines on magistrum.net. The objective is straightforward: help a business digitise sensibly using the right technology, sequenced in the right order, before any major build or buy commitment.

## What's included

- Discovery and current-state assessment of the existing systems and processes.
- Technology advice: which platforms (Zoho, custom build, off-the-shelf SaaS) fit which problem.
- Sequencing: what to deploy first, what to defer, where the dependencies are.
- Vendor and tool evaluation, including total cost of ownership and exit cost.
- Roadmap and budget planning over a 6 to 18 month horizon.
- Light project management to keep the implementation honest if Magistrum or a third party delivers.

## How we work

The practice draws on a finance-led co-founder (Haridas Krishna, over 25 years in finance across the Middle East and India) and an IT-led co-founder (Seema Krishna, Oracle Certified IT professional). That pairing keeps the consultancy grounded in real operational and financial constraints rather than abstract tech advice.

## Common questions

### Are you vendor-neutral?

Within the Zoho ecosystem we are an Authorised Partner. Outside Zoho, we are happy to recommend the right tool for the job, including non-Zoho options when those are a better fit. The discovery deliverable always includes the reasoning.

### Will you actually deliver the work, or just advise?

Either. Some clients want consultancy only and execute themselves. Others want us to deliver, especially for Zoho implementation or software development.

### How long is a typical consultancy engagement?

From a half-day clarifier through to multi-week scoping engagements. We agree the scope and a fixed price up front.
