## Overview

Software Development is one of Magistrum's five service lines on magistrum.net. The team builds custom software for short-term and long-term business needs, with a stated focus on highly scalable solutions that keep infrastructure complexity and operational cost low.

## What's included

- Discovery and scope, including the case for build vs. buy.
- Architecture and technology selection with future cost in mind.
- Front-end and back-end development, with API and integration work where needed.
- Quality assurance and user-acceptance testing.
- Release and post-release support.
- Where it fits, integration into the wider Zoho stack already implemented for the client.

## How we work

We avoid over-engineering. The team optimises for solutions that perform well across business endpoints without dragging in disproportionate infrastructure or operational overhead.

## Common questions

### Do you build custom apps inside Zoho, or only outside it?

Both. Zoho Creator low-code apps are a frequent extension for clients already on the Zoho stack. Standalone custom applications are also delivered.

### Do you do mobile apps?

Yes, when they are the right answer to the business problem. We will say so honestly during discovery if a responsive web app or progressive web app would do the job at lower lifetime cost.

### How is pricing structured?

Fixed scope, fixed price for well-defined work. Time and materials when the scope is genuinely exploratory, with weekly reviews and clear stop points.
