## Overview

Zoho Books Certification Training. Authorised Zoho training covering Books fundamentals through advanced topics, with UAE VAT and Corporate Tax built into the curriculum. For finance teams and individual accountants.

## What's included

- Zoho Books fundamentals (chart of accounts, masters, transactions)
- Advanced features (workflows, approvals, multi-currency, multi-branch)
- UAE VAT 201 walkthroughs
- Corporate Tax filing in Zoho Books
- Case-based assessments
- Authorised certificate on completion
- Lifetime access to course material

## Typical timeline

20 to 40 hours of instruction, delivered over 2 to 4 weeks. Online live sessions, or in-person at our Dubai centre.

## What it costs

Individual rate: AED 1,200 to AED 2,500 depending on programme depth. Team rates are available for groups of 4+.

## Common questions

### Is this a recognised certification?

Yes — we're a Zoho Authorised Training Institute for Zoho Finance Suite, including Books. Certificates are co-branded and verifiable.

### Do you place students in jobs?

Our parent practice in India offers "100% Lifetime Job Assurance" on training programmes. In the UAE, we maintain a network of partner firms hiring Zoho-skilled accountants — placement is best-effort, not contractual in the UAE entity.

### Can my whole team train together?

Yes. We run private cohorts for finance teams. Useful when you're about to roll out Zoho Books and want the team certified before go-live.
