## Overview

Zoho Books Implementation. End-to-end setup configured for UAE VAT and Corporate Tax compliance. Most-requested engagement.

## What's included

- Discovery call (free 45 minutes) and written scope
- Chart of accounts design for your business
- UAE VAT (5%) and Corporate Tax (9%) tax-code configuration
- FTA-compliant tax invoice templates, bilingual EN/AR
- Bank feeds and automated reconciliation rules
- Customer, supplier and item master data migration
- Opening balances tied out against your prior books or auditor's balances
- Workflow + approval matrix configured to your hierarchy
- Team training, typically 3 hours for finance, recorded
- 30 days post-go-live support

## Typical timeline

7-14 working days for most UAE SMEs. Simple setups can go live in 5 days; complex multi-branch migrations can run 3-4 weeks.

## What it costs

Implementation typically runs AED 5,500 to AED 18,000 depending on scope. The Zoho Books licence itself is paid directly to Zoho (from around AED 60 per user per month). We confirm a fixed-scope quote in writing after the discovery call — no scope creep.

## Common questions

### Is Zoho Books compliant with UAE VAT?

Yes — completely. We configure the standard rated, zero rated, exempt and out-of-scope codes during setup, plus Designated Zone treatment if your business sits in one.

### Is it ready for UAE Corporate Tax (9%)?

Yes. We map your chart of accounts against CT treatment from day one, including disallowance tags on relevant expense GLs and the Small Business Relief flag if your annual revenue is under AED 3M.

### Can you migrate from Tally / QuickBooks / Sage / Excel?

These are our four most-common starting points. Master data is straightforward; we tie out closing balances against your prior period to make sure nothing was lost in translation.

### What happens after go-live?

30 days of support are included. After that, optional retainers cover month-end close support, VAT 201 and Corporate Tax filing, and ongoing optimisation.
