## Overview

Zoho CRM Implementation. Configured around your actual sales process, not a generic template. A pipeline the team will use, not abandon.

## What's included

- Sales-process mapping workshop (typically half a day)
- Custom pipeline stages, fields, and automation rules
- Lead source tracking and lead scoring
- Email integration (IMAP/OAuth) and WhatsApp Business integration where available
- Approval flows for discount / quote / contract steps if needed
- Role-based dashboards for reps, managers, and leadership
- Mobile app rollout for field-based teams
- Training across roles
- 30 days adoption support

## Typical timeline

10 to 21 working days. Depends mainly on how custom the sales process is.

## What it costs

Typically AED 7,500 to AED 22,000. The Zoho CRM licence is paid to Zoho (from around AED 80 per user per month for Standard tier). Fixed-scope quote provided after discovery.

## Common questions

### Can you integrate Zoho CRM with Zoho Books?

Yes, natively. Customers, invoices and payments sync without any custom code.

### Can you migrate from HubSpot / Salesforce / Pipedrive?

Yes. We export deals, contacts, accounts and activities from the source, clean the data, and import to Zoho CRM. Sometimes a small data cleanup workshop is worth the time before migration.

### Will the team actually adopt it?

That's where most CRM rollouts fail. Our methodology emphasises mapping the real sales process (not the one in the slide deck) and training by role. We come back at 30 days for an adoption review.
