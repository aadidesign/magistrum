## Overview

Zoho Finance Suite — Books, Invoice, Expense, Inventory and Payroll — implemented as one connected stack. Cross-module workflows so an expense claim hits the right GL automatically, inventory movements update revenue, and payroll posts to your books at the click of a button.

## What's included

- All five Finance Suite modules configured to your business
- Cross-module workflow design (e.g. Inventory → Books revenue recognition)
- Approval matrices configured to your real hierarchy
- FTA-compliant tax handling across the stack
- Multi-currency and multi-branch setup if needed
- Training across finance, ops, and sales teams
- 30 days post-go-live support

## Typical timeline

3 to 5 weeks for a typical UAE SME. The complexity is in the cross-module design, not any one app.

## What it costs

Typically AED 18,000 to AED 45,000. The full Suite licence runs from around AED 250 per user per month from Zoho.

## Common questions

### Do I need the whole Suite or just Books?

If you're an early-stage SME with simple invoicing and no inventory, start with Books. If you have inventory, multi-currency operations, frequent expense claims and payroll, the Suite is usually worth it.

### Can I add modules later?

Yes, easily. Each module is independent but plays well with the others. Some clients start with Books + Invoice and add Inventory + Expense + Payroll later.
