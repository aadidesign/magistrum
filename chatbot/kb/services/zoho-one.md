## Overview

Zoho One Implementation — the full 45+ application stack, configured as one coherent system with single sign-on, unified data, and shared workflow logic. The "all-in" choice for SMEs ready to consolidate.

## What's included

- Roadmap workshop — which apps to deploy first
- Each chosen app configured to your business processes
- Single sign-on across all apps
- Reporting layer with cross-app insights
- Phased team training
- 6-12 week rollout in phases

## Typical timeline

6 to 12 weeks, in three to four phases.

## What it costs

Implementation typically AED 35,000 to AED 95,000 depending on which apps and how custom. The Zoho One licence runs from around AED 165 per user per month — extremely good value if you use even half the stack.

## Common questions

### Which apps are most-used by UAE SMEs?

CRM, Books, Desk (support), Projects, People (HR), Workplace (mail + collaboration), and Inventory. The rest are situational.

### Can we deploy in phases?

Yes — that's how we always recommend it. Phase 1 is usually CRM + Books + Workplace; Phase 2 adds Inventory + Expense; Phase 3 adds Desk + People + Projects.
