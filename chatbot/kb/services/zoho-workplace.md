## Overview

Zoho Workplace Implementation, including Google Workspace to Zoho Workplace migration. Moving off Google or Microsoft 365 to a cost-effective, privacy-focused collaboration stack.

## What's included

- Mail, calendar and contact migration with zero downtime
- File migration with original folder structure preserved
- MX and DNS cutover (we handle the records)
- User onboarding sessions for your team
- Mobile device configuration (iOS and Android)
- Post-cutover monitoring for the first week

## Typical timeline

5 to 10 working days, depending on user count and mailbox sizes.

## What it costs

Typically AED 4,500 to AED 12,000 for the migration project. Zoho Workplace licences run from around AED 18 per user per month — significantly less than Google Workspace Business Standard.

## Common questions

### Will my email be down during the migration?

No. We migrate mail in parallel to the existing system, then cut over MX records at a low-traffic window (usually a Saturday night). Users wake up Sunday morning on the new system with all their history intact.

### Will my email addresses change?

No. Your domain stays the same. Only the underlying email platform changes.

### Why move off Google Workspace?

Most clients move for cost savings, privacy-focused infrastructure, or to consolidate inside the Zoho stack alongside CRM and Books. Performance and reliability are comparable.
